AR Particle Systems Thesis

Thesis pushing the boundaries of GPGPU computing on a HoloLens

This thesis is an exploration of the capabilities of an untethered AR device to run particle systems. Particle systems are critical to achieve many effects in graphics, but they can be notoriously costly to run. This thesis examines what a HoloLens can currently run without dropping below 60 fps

  • Date: Fall 2017 to Spring 2018

It's a Small World

A highly detailed 3D model made with Maya using the Arnold Renderer

Fun Fact: If you look closely, you'll notice that everything that spins in the scene has a motor and a wire to power it! This is a photorealistic 3D model of the "It's a Small World" ride in Disneyland. The project was created in Maya. Almost everything in the scene uses procedural texturing. It was rendered using an Arnold renderer

  • Date: Fall 2017

OpenGL Ocean

A realistic underwater scene made with OpenGL

An underwater scene created using OpenGL and written in C/C++. The coral, bubbles, and seaweed are 2D textures with transparency, the coral tubes and oysters are created with native OpenGL geometry, and the fish and crab are obj files. The scene contains underwater caustics as well

  • Date: Fall 2017

The Trash Panda Dilemma

Platformer game using Java letting you live the life of vermin on a mission

Vermin everywhere are sick and tired of the reputation they've gotten as pests! Now they've teamed up to help improve the public image of “pests” everywhere however they can. Together, their abilities make them the perfect group to gather coins for the humans that have fallen down into the sewers. In this side-scroller, platformer game, you can play as a raccoon, who is able to claw through obstacles, an opossum who can play dead in the face of foes so they leave you alone, and a rat who can squeeze into those hard to reach spaces. Gather as many coins as you can while avoiding foes, poisons, and traps. Pick up hats along the way to look cute to the humans

  • Date: Fall 2017

AR Mini Golf

A mini golf game using spatial mapping to place obstacles in your room

This was an exploration of the spatial understanding API's for the HoloLens. The game scans your room and determines suitable locations on the floor to place obstacles in order to play mini golf

  • Date: Spring 2017

AR Musical Rain

Catch the musical notes before they touch the ground in your living room

My first Unity project, this game scans your room to detect the ceiling and floor height. It then places a cloud on the ceiling that rains musical notes. The users have to catch them before they hit the floor. Updates made to the game after the video was taken include special power ups that help the user to catch the notes

  • Date: Fall 2016
